Start Here

Welcome to the What Really Happened™ Master Curriculum. This guide will help you understand the program structure and how to begin your journey.

Core Purpose

WRH teaches trauma responses, addiction patterns, freeze responses, shame cycles, and survival behavior as predictable mechanisms — not character flaws. This is educational, not therapeutic, and requires no personal disclosure to participate.

🎓 Non-Clinical

Psychoeducation focused on understanding, not diagnosis or treatment.

🔒 Private

No disclosure of personal trauma is required for participation.

📊 Evidence-Based

Grounded in neuroscience, trauma research, and behavioral adaptation theory.

🎯 Practical

Designed for real-world application in high-barrier environments.

Curriculum Structure

The WRH Master Curriculum is organized into four comprehensive parts, each building on the previous:

Part I: Survival Wiring

26 sessions on trauma literacy, survival responses, and neurobiological adaptation.

Part II: Institutional Navigation

30 sessions on system interaction, reentry, and behavioral management.

Part III: Resilience & Agency

19 sessions on autonomy restoration and cross-system integration.

Part IV: Facilitation

Toolkit, protocols, safety frameworks, and certification pathways.

Ready to Begin?

Start with Part I to build your foundational understanding of survival wiring and trauma literacy. Each session builds progressively, so sequential learning is recommended.
