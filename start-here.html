Start Here: WRH Pilot Overview

WRH is structured non-clinical survival-mode education with safety boundaries and measurable pilot delivery.

WRH helps agencies teach survival-mode patterns in plain language for high-barrier groups such as reentry, diversion, recovery support, veteran peer groups, and court-ordered settings.

Boundary: WRH is not therapy, diagnosis, treatment, crisis counseling, or disclosure-based trauma processing.

Use: a bounded pilot tests whether the material can be delivered clearly, safely, and usefully in one setting
