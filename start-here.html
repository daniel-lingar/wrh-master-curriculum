Core Purpose

WRH teaches trauma responses, addiction patterns, freeze responses, shame cycles, and survival behavior as predictable mechanisms — not character flaws.

Important Boundaries

  • This is non-clinical psychoeducation
  • No disclosure of personal trauma is required
  • Not therapy, not medical advice
  • Designed for group delivery in recovery, reentry, veteran, and peer support settings
