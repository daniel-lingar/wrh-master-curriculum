About WRH Master Curriculum

Our Mission

What Really Happened™ (WRH) is a non-clinical, survival-mode education program designed to support individuals navigating complex systems and trauma recovery. We provide structured, evidence-informed curriculum for reentry, diversion, recovery support, veterans, and high-barrier populations.

Our mission is to deliver measurable, safe, and effective educational interventions that build survival literacy, resilience, and agency without crossing into clinical treatment or diagnosis.

What We Do

WRH Master Curriculum provides:

  • Survival Literacy Education: Understanding trauma responses, freeze states, and survival wiring
  • Institutional Navigation: Strategies for reentry, system interaction, and complex environment management
  • Resilience Building: Frameworks for autonomy restoration and behavioral-institutional integration
  • Facilitator Training: Comprehensive toolkit for safe, effective program delivery
  • Safety Protocols: Clear crisis management, escalation procedures, and participant protection

What We Are NOT

WRH is explicitly not:

  • Therapy or clinical treatment
  • Diagnostic in nature
  • A replacement for mental health services
  • Medical or psychiatric intervention

Participants are encouraged to maintain relationships with their existing clinical providers while engaging with WRH education.

Our Approach
