# Sales System

## Dark Mode Executive Edition

The WRH Sales System is the structured communication layer that supports agencies, partners, and procurement officers as they move through the WRH decision process.

It is not marketing.

It is not persuasion.

It is not pressure.

It is a professional, predictable, high-clarity communication system that helps agencies understand:

- what WRH is
- what WRH delivers
- how WRH works
- what they can purchase
- how implementation unfolds
- how outcomes are measured

The Sales System includes:

- Attachment Sequence
- Buyer Journey
- Follow-Up Scripts

Each component supports a different stage of agency engagement.

---

## 1. Attachment Sequence

The Attachment Sequence is the structured order in which WRH materials are sent to a buyer.

It ensures that:

- the right documents arrive at the right time
- the buyer is never overwhelmed
- the buyer always knows what they are looking at
- the narrative builds logically
- the agency sees WRH as a system, not a product

A typical sequence includes:

1. WRH One-Page Overview
2. WRH Master Document
3. Product Catalog
4. Pilot Program Sheet
5. Pricing Sheet
6. Statement of Work
7. Evaluation Framework
8. Session 16 Sample Packet

This sequence mirrors the Buyer-Facing System and aligns with how agencies make decisions.

---

## 2. Buyer Journey

The Buyer Journey maps how agencies move from first contact to contract.

It includes:

- initial interest
- informational meeting
- document review
- internal discussion
- procurement review
- scope alignment
- pricing confirmation
- contract drafting
- implementation scheduling

The Buyer Journey is not linear.

Agencies move back and forth between stages.

The WRH Sales System provides:

- clarity
- predictability
- structure
- professional tone
- consistent messaging

This reduces friction and accelerates decision-making.

---

## 3. Follow-Up Scripts

Follow-Up Scripts are not sales scripts.

They are professional communication templates that ensure:

- clarity
- consistency
- accuracy
- alignment
- tone discipline

Scripts include:

- post-meeting summaries
- document delivery messages
- clarification responses
- procurement guidance
- next-step confirmations
- timeline alignment messages
- evaluation explanation messages

These scripts help agencies feel supported, not pressured.

They also ensure WRH communication is:

- high-authority
- concise
- structured
- emotionally intelligent
- aligned with public-sector expectations

---

## Why the Sales System Matters

The Sales System:

- reduces confusion
- reduces friction
- reduces delays
- increases clarity
- increases trust
- increases alignment
- increases contract readiness

It ensures that agencies experience WRH as:

- a system
- a structure
- a professional partner
- a predictable process

This is essential for public-sector adoption.

## Summary Line

The WRH Sales System provides a structured, professional communication pathway that helps agencies understand WRH, evaluate WRH, and move confidently toward implementation.
