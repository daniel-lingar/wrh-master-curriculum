Pilot Package

Information on pilot program implementation, metrics, and partnership opportunities for the WRH Master Curriculum.

Overview

The WRH Master Curriculum offers pilot packages for organizations interested in implementing our unique psychoeducational program. These packages are designed to facilitate seamless integration, provide comprehensive support, and enable data-driven evaluation of program effectiveness.

What's Included

Each pilot package is tailored to the needs of your organization but typically includes:

  • Full access to the WRH Master Curriculum content.
  • Facilitator training and certification.
  • Implementation guides and best practices.
  • Access to our metrics tracking and reporting tools.
  • Dedicated support from the Capitol Contracts LLC team.
  • Customizable program materials and branding options.

Metrics & Evaluation

Our pilot programs emphasize measurable outcomes. We work with your organization to define key performance indicators (KPIs) and provide tools to track participant engagement, understanding, and perceived impact. This data helps demonstrate the value of the WRH Master Curriculum and informs continuous program improvement.

Partnership Opportunities

We are actively seeking partnerships with organizations committed to fostering resilience and supporting individuals in high-barrier environments. If you believe the WRH Master Curriculum aligns with your mission, we encourage you to reach out to discuss collaboration.
