Capability Statement

Capitol Contracts LLC offers the WRH Master Curriculum as a structured, replicable, non-clinical psychoeducational program.

Company Overview

Capitol Contracts LLC specializes in providing high-impact, non-clinical education and training solutions for high-barrier populations. Our flagship offering, the What Really Happened (WRH) Master Curriculum, is designed to bridge the gap between survival-mode behaviors and successful institutional integration.

The WRH Framework

Our curriculum is built on a four-part framework that addresses the root causes of survival-mode behaviors and provides practical strategies for change.

Survival Wiring

Understanding the neurobiology of threat and the mechanics of the freeze response.

System Logic

Decoding institutional environments and navigating complex social systems.

Resilience

Building agency and autonomy through structured behavioral frameworks.

Facilitation

Standardized delivery models with rigorous safety and crisis protocols.

WRH Master Curriculum

Non-clinical survival-mode education with safety boundaries and measurable pilot delivery.
