Capability Statement

Comprehensive overview of Capitol Contracts LLC and the WRH Master Curriculum.

Core Competencies

Capitol Contracts LLC specializes in developing and delivering innovative educational programs focused on survival literacy and resilience. Our core competencies include:

  • Curriculum Development: Designing evidence-informed, non-clinical psychoeducational content.
  • Program Implementation: Strategic deployment of educational programs in diverse environments.
  • Facilitator Training: Equipping professionals with the tools to deliver our curriculum effectively.
  • Impact Measurement: Utilizing data-driven approaches to assess program effectiveness and outcomes.
  • Behavioral Science Integration: Applying insights from neuroscience and psychology to practical education.

Differentiators

What sets the WRH Master Curriculum apart:

  • Non-Clinical Focus: Provides education without requiring personal disclosure or clinical diagnosis.
  • Adaptability: Designed for high-barrier environments, including reentry and diversion programs.
  • Measurable Outcomes: Emphasis on pilot programs with trackable metrics for continuous improvement.
  • Comprehensive Approach: Addresses trauma responses, addiction patterns, and survival behaviors holistically.
  • Expert-Led Development: Curriculum developed by leading experts in behavioral science and trauma-informed care.

Past Performance

Capitol Contracts LLC has a proven track record of successful program development and delivery. Our pilot programs have demonstrated significant improvements in participant engagement and understanding of survival literacy concepts. Specific case studies and references are available upon request.
