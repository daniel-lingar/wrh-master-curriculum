# Capitol Contracts LLC — Mission and Role

Capitol Contracts LLC is the business and implementation company behind WRH.

WRH is the curriculum.

Capitol Contracts LLC is the company that packages, protects, presents, and delivers it to organizations.

## Relationship Between the Parts

- *From the Storm to the Fire* is the lived source story.
- WRH is the education system built from the patterns.
- Capitol Contracts LLC is the company that brings that system to partners, programs, agencies, and buyers.

## What Capitol Contracts Is

Capitol Contracts LLC is the public-facing business container for structured, non-clinical psychoeducational materials, curriculum systems, implementation tools, and training frameworks.

Its audiences include:

- recovery programs
- veteran-support programs
- reentry programs
- peer-support groups
- community agencies
- institutional education partners
- behavioral-health-adjacent programs
- support staff who need better language
- organizations where trauma behavior is often misunderstood

Its flagship education system is What Really Happened / WRH.

WRH teaches trauma responses, addiction patterns, freeze responses, shame cycles, nervous-system adaptation, and survival behavior as understandable mechanisms instead of character failures.

## Mission

Capitol Contracts LLC exists to provide structured, non-clinical education that helps people and organizations understand survival behavior before they misread it as laziness, defiance, weakness, manipulation, noncompliance, or moral failure.

In plain language:

> Capitol Contracts helps organizations teach what is happening underneath the behavior.

Not to excuse harm.

Not to replace therapy.

Not to diagnose anyone.

But to give people and systems better language before shame, punishment, relapse, shutdown, or crisis takes over.

## Goal

The goal is to turn WRH into something real organizations can use.

Capitol Contracts gives organizations a curriculum that says:

> Before you punish the behavior, understand the survival pattern.

That does not remove accountability.

It makes accountability more possible because the person can finally see the loop.

## What Capitol Contracts Provides

Capitol Contracts may provide:

1. WRH Master Curriculum — the full 75/76-session system.
2. WRH Pilot Deployment Package — a shorter, field-ready implementation version.
3. Top 10 Core — a low-barrier introduction program.
4. Facilitator scripts and support materials.
5. Participant education materials.
6. Implementation support.
7. Capability and procurement materials.

## What Capitol Contracts Is Not

Capitol Contracts is not a therapy provider.

It is not medical care.

It is not legal advice.

It is not diagnosis.

It is not crisis care.

It is not a substitute for licensed clinical treatment.

That boundary protects the company, facilitators, host sites, and participants.

## Official Role

Capitol Contracts is the business layer.

Its job is to hold:

- capability statements
- procurement-safe company language
- service descriptions
- leadership/control language
- readiness roadmaps
- pilot evidence templates
- public business documentation

## Mission Statement

Capitol Contracts LLC develops and delivers structured, non-clinical psychoeducational curriculum systems that help recovery, reentry, veteran-support, peer-support, and institutional education partners recognize survival patterns, reduce shame-based misreadings, preserve accountability, and teach safer interruption points.

## Raw Mission

Capitol Contracts exists to get WRH into the rooms where people are being misunderstood, punished, written off, or lost because nobody taught them what survival mode looks like.

If people can see the pattern, they have a chance to interrupt it.
