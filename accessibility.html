Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to ensuring digital accessibility for everyone.

Commitment to Accessibility

Capitol Contracts LLC is committed to making its website and digital content accessible to all individuals, including those with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Measures to Support Accessibility

We take the following measures to ensure accessibility of the WRH Master Curriculum website:

  • Include accessibility as part of our mission statement.
  • Include accessibility throughout our internal policies.
  • Provide continual accessibility training for our staff.
  • Employ clear and consistent navigation across the site.
  • Ensure content is readable and understandable.
  • Use appropriate color contrast for text and background elements.
  • Provide alternative text for all meaningful images.
  • Ensure keyboard navigation is fully functional.

Conformance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA. The WRH Master Curriculum website is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 Level AA. Partially conformant means that some parts of the content do not yet fully conform to the accessibility standard.

Technical Specifications

Accessibility of the WRH Master Curriculum website relies on the following technologies to work with the particular combination of web browser and any assistive technologies or plugins installed on your computer:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

These technologies are relied upon for conformance with the accessibility standards used.
