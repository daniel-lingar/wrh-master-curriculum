Accessibility Statement (Section 508)

Capitol Contracts LLC is committed to ensuring that the "What Really Happened" (WRH) Master Curriculum portal and its associated digital materials are accessible to all individuals, including those with disabilities.

Section 508 Compliance

We strive to conform to the Revised Section 508 Standards of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, as well as the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. Our goal is to provide a user experience that is functional and accessible to the widest possible audience.

Key Accessibility Features

  • Semantic HTML: We use proper HTML structure to ensure compatibility with screen readers and assistive technologies.
  • Text Alternatives: Images and non-text content include descriptive alt-text.
  • Keyboard Navigation: All interactive elements are designed to be accessible via keyboard alone.
  • Contrast and Readability: We maintain high color contrast ratios and legible typography for improved readability.

Contact Information

If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us. We are dedicated to resolving any issues to ensure full access to our procurement and clinical safety documentation.
