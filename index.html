Purpose

Built to explain survival mode without clinical fog.

WRH translates trauma language into direct, teachable structure. It is designed for education, peer support, justice-involved settings, veteran-facing organizations, recovery support, and high-barrier communities.

Plain Language

The curriculum avoids jargon and explains nervous-system survival responses in words people can use while dysregulated.

Non-Clinical Delivery

WRH is educational. It is not therapy, diagnosis, treatment, crisis counseling, or medical advice.

Facilitator-Ready

The structure is intended for consistent delivery, repeatable sessions, handouts, implementation support, and organizational review.
